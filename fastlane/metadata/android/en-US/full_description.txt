Foreign language notebook. An application that will help you learn foreign languages by composing phrases in a foreign language as if it were your own. It is possible to save phrases, as well as convenient tools for filtering saved phrases. There is support for speech-to-text recognition as well as text-to-speech. Completely built on free and open source tools. Practice your sentence writing skills in: English, Arabic, Azerbaijani, Catalan, Chinese, Czech, Danish, Dutch, Esperanto, Finnish, French, German, Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Indonesian, Irish, Italian, Japanese, Korean , Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Slovak, Spanish, Swedish, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian.
